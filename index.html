MY DREAM DESTINATION

is Switzerland!


What is a dream Destination?
It is the desire of getting to a destination and your need to accomplish or obtain it.

I have visited many places around the world like Thailand, Italy, Rome, France etc... I am very fortunate
that I got to visit my dream destination in the summer of 2017 in a family trip to europe.
Switzerland was very beautiful... I remember sitting in the train admirmiring the beauty of the green landscape
in front of me. I just loved it, it was also the very first time I had seen snow so it was very special for me.
I think Switzerland was my mom and dad's dream destination as well... we had a lot of fun there, this sounded like
a perfect vacation but due to the cold me and my mom got fever... but we recovered soon and that didn't stop us
from having an amazing time together. This was my dream destination.
